Mushroom Congee

Warm and healthy... Mushroom Congee!

Great hearty and warm recipe. Put in a pot, set a timer, come back when it's done!

Busy working from home? Watching kids? Or your favorite series on Netflix? *wink wink*

This simple yet delightful in both taste and convenience is a great all rounder meal (or snack) for everyone!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Uncooked Rice of your choice
  • 1 thumb or 1 tablespoon Peeled Ginger
  • 100g Fresh Shitake Mushrooms or 5-6 pieces Dried Shitake
  • 2 Cups Stock (e.g. Chicken, Vegetable) of your choice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • OPTIONAL: Scallions and/or Fried Garlic for garnish

Step-by-Step

Prep Work

  1. Soak Rice in water for 30 minutes
  2. Cut Shitake Mushrooms into desired shape (if using dried Shitake, soak in hot water for 5-10 minutes before cutting)
  3. Drain water from rice. Rinse and drain one more time
  4. Saute mushrooms and ginger in deep pot for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add drained uncooked rice. Continue to cook, constantly stirring, for another 5 minutes
  6. Add 2 cups water. Bring to a boil. Let it boil for around 10 minutes uncovered
  7. Add stock of choice, bring heat to low cover and let simmer for 20-30 minutes
  8. Salt and pepper to taste
  9. Top with Scallions and/or Fried Garlic and serve
