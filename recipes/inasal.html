Chicken Inasal

Filipino style grilled chicken marinated in vinegar and calamansi.

Originating from the Visayas Region of the Philippines, Chicken Inasal is a simple dish that makes you want to come back for more!

This recipe is a variation of the famous dish catered to my families palette. Minimal, easily accessible ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Chicken thighs or any dark meat
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • 1 cup coconut vinegar or white cane vinegar
  • 3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 stem lemongrass (leaves included)
  • 10 Calamansi or 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons Salt
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground Black Pepper

Step-by-Step

Prep work

  1. Combine Vinegar, Soy Sauce, Calamansi (remove seeds and include skin in mixture), black pepper.
  2. Cut (optionally smash) the lemon grass (leaves included) into 1 inch strips and add to liquid mixture.
  3. Cut (recommended smash) the garlic (set aside some of the garlic skin/peel) and add to liquid mixture.
  4. Rub salt throughly onto chicken.
  5. Add the chicken to the marinate, mix well, and let marinate for at least 2 hours.

Cooking

  1. Remove Chicken from marinade (set marinade aside) and remove any garlic and lemongrass residue from the chicken.
  2. Grill over medium fire, basting using marinade until internal temperature is between 175°-190°.
  3. Let rest for at least 5 minutes before serving.
  4. OPTIONAL: Serve with Soy Sauce, Calamansi, and Chili.
